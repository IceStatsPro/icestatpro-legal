Privacy Policy
Last updated: May 2026

IceStat Pro ("we", "our", or "the service") is a statistical fish behaviour analytics tool accessible via Telegram. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

By using IceStat Pro, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

From Telegram:

  • Your Telegram numeric user ID
  • Your Telegram display name (first name)
  • Your Telegram username (if set)

From your usage:

  • Fish sighting entries you log manually
  • Session start and end times
  • Subscription plan and status
  • Payment records (plan name, Stars amount, date)

2. INFORMATION WE DO NOT COLLECT

  • No passwords or authentication credentials
  • No financial or banking details
  • No location or GPS data
  • No contact lists or phone numbers
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No browsing history or cookies
  • No messages from your Telegram chats

3. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

  • To authenticate you when you open the app
  • To assign and maintain your subscription role
  • To store fish sighting logs for statistical analysis
  • To process and verify payments via Telegram Stars
  • To respond to support or feedback messages

4. DATA STORAGE

Local storage: Fish logs and session data are stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. This data does not leave your device unless you explicitly export it.

Server storage: Your account information (Telegram ID, role, subscription status) and payment records are stored on our secure server hosted on Render (render.com).

Retention: Account data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

5. DATA SHARING

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties.

The only third-party service involved is Telegram, which handles identity verification and payment processing via Telegram Stars. Telegram's own privacy policy applies to data processed through their platform.

6. PAYMENTS

All payments are processed exclusively through Telegram Stars. We do not have access to your payment card details, bank information, or any financial credentials.

Payment records we store include: plan name, number of Stars paid, and transaction date. These are used solely for subscription management and support purposes.

All purchases are final and non-refundable. By completing a purchase you acknowledge and agree that no refunds will be issued under any circumstances.

7. YOUR RIGHTS

  • Access: You can request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate data
  • Portability: You can export your fish log data at any time via the app's export feature

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via Telegram at @IceStatProBot.

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

IceStat Pro is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

9. DISCLAIMER

IceStat Pro is a statistical analysis and educational tool designed to analyse fish behaviour patterns based on historical data.

All analysis and estimates provided are for educational and informational purposes only. Results are based on historical pattern data and do not constitute any form of guarantee, advice, or assured outcome.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of IceStat Pro after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
